The Golden GIFT of GOD is His LOVE for us.
#1
Miracle
Jesus teaches us humility, despite being praised and worship, admits and knows who really is the only good in this world. That which, Jesus himself being close to GOD, KNOWS the heart of god.
Tinuruan tayo ni Hesus ng pagpakumbaba, kahit Siya'y pinapapurihan at sinasamba, ay binanggit at alam kung sino talaga ang mabuti sa mundong ito. Kahit si Hesus ay malapit sa Dios, ay alam ang puso ng Dios.
Luke 18 : 18 - 20 Lucas 18 : 18 - 20
18 And a certain ruler asked him, saying,

Good Master,

 what shall I do to inherit eternal life?
18 At tinanong siya ng isang pinuno, na sinasabi,

Mabuting Guro,

 anong gagawin ko upang magmana ng walang hanggang buhay?
19 And Jesus said unto him,
Why callest thou me good?

none is good,
save one,

that is, God.
19 At sinabi sa kaniya ni Jesus,
Bakit mo ako tinatawag na mabuti?

walang mabuti,
kundi isa,

ang Dios lamang.
20 Thou knowest the commandments, Do not commit adultery, Do not kill, Do not steal, Do not bear false witness, Honour thy father and thy mother.
